water to milk 1:2

part 1
1 cup water
1 tsp black tea (ie wagh bakri brand)
1 black peppercorn
1 clove
1 sprig mint
1 inch ginger grated
10 cardamom pods crushed

part 2
2 cups milk or alternatives

instructions

Put everything in part 1 into a pot and boil for 5 mins.
Add the milk and heat until lightly boiling.
Serve.
